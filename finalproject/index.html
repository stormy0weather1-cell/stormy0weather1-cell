Space Hobby Journal

I keep a small journal about what I learn from space documentaries and astronomy nights. This site is a simple place to share that hobby and the images I like to revisit.

The gallery page includes the largest collection of images, and the about page explains how I organize my notes each week.

Why I Like This Hobby

Space stories mix science, history, and a little imagination. It is relaxing to read, and it makes my weekend walks feel more connected to the night sky.
